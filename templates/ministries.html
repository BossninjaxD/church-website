Our Ministries

Serving our church, our community, and beyond.

Our church offers a variety of ministries for all ages and walks of life. Whether you’re looking to deepen your faith, serve the community, or find a place to connect, there’s a ministry for you.

Worship Ministry

Join our worship team in leading praise and worship during Sunday services.

Children’s Ministry

Helping children grow in their faith through Sunday school, VBS, and fun activities.

Youth Ministry

Guiding middle and high school students in faith, friendship, and service.

Community Outreach

Serving the local community through food drives, support groups, and outreach events.

Life Groups

Small groups meeting throughout the week to study God’s Word and support one another.

Prayer Ministry

Join our team in praying for the needs of the church and our community.

Men’s & Women’s Ministries

Fellowship and study groups specifically for men and women to grow in their faith.

Missions

Supporting missionaries and global outreach efforts to spread the Gospel worldwide.
